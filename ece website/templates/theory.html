HCL Comnet Interview Experience - 21st November
Posted by: A D ❤️ (10)
IT WAS A VERY NICE EXPERIENCE with HCL Comnet. It was the first interview I gave.

The selection procedure was 5 rounds on our campus.

The first-round elimination was based on the AMCAT results. You must have good grades in order to get selected through this round.

The second round of elimination was based on the grammar test. It is quite easy.

The third round was the GI (group interview), or you can say that your communication skills are evaluated in this round.

The fourth round was the Technical Interview based on networking, your major projects, and C++.

The final round was the HR round. The HR was a lady. She was very friendly. She asked about the questions based on your resume. So make sure that you should write only about your strengths.
