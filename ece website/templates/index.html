Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

About the ECE Department

The Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) Department at Kongu Engineering College is dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in the field of electronics and communication. It offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for careers in diverse areas such as telecommunications, embedded systems, signal processing, and VLSI design. Our state-of-the-art laboratories and experienced faculty provide a solid foundation for students to explore emerging technologies and contribute to groundbreaking research.

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in engineering education.

Mission

To foster a culture of innovation and provide a supportive environment for all students.

HOD's Message

We are dedicated to providing quality education and preparing our students for a successful future.

Program Outcomes

  1. Engineering Knowledge: Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering to solve complex problems.
  2. Problem Analysis: Identify, formulate, and analyze engineering problems.
  3. Design and Development of Solutions: Design solutions for complex engineering problems.
  4. Investigation: Conduct investigations using appropriate methods.
  5. Modern Tool Usage: Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools to complex engineering contexts.
  6. The Engineer and Society: Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues associated with professional engineering practice.
  7. Environment and Sustainability: Understand the impact of professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate knowledge of sustainable development.
  8. Ethics: Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities in engineering practice.
  9. Individual and Team Work: Function effectively as an individual or as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.
  10. Communication: Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large.

About Our HOD

Dr. N. Kasthuri is the esteemed Head of the Electronics and Communication Engineering department at Kongu Engineering College. She holds a BE, ME, and PhD, with her expertise primarily focused on Signal and Image Processing, Embedded Systems, and Soft Computing. Dr. Kasthuri joined KEC on July 14, 1999, and has accumulated over 22 years of teaching experience.She has significantly contributed to academia, with 59 papers published and 50 papers presented at various national and international conferences. Additionally, she has a notable record of publishing in both national and international journals, including works such as "Electronic Jacquard Based Handloom with I2C Enabled Client-Server Architecture" and "Woven Design Data Transmission using TTL Logic for 128 Hooks Electronic Cardless Jacquard.Dr. Kasthuri's dedication to fostering innovation through research and collaboration makes her a pivotal figure in shaping the future of engineering education at KEC.
